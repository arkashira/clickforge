```markdown
# Customer Journey Map for Clickforge

## Phase 1: Aware
- **Trigger Event**: Tech professionals encounter limitations with existing browser automation tools during their workflows.
- **Friction Points**: Lack of awareness about Clickforge; existing tools do not meet their advanced automation needs.
- **User Emotions**: Frustration with current solutions; curiosity about potential alternatives.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Create targeted marketing campaigns showcasing Clickforge's unique features; leverage testimonials from early adopters.
- **Metric**: Number of website visits and engagement with marketing content (e.g., blog posts, webinars).

---

## Phase 2: Consider
- **Trigger Event**: Users actively search for browser automation solutions after experiencing limitations.
- **Friction Points**: Overwhelmed by the number of options; uncertainty about Clickforge's capabilities compared to competitors.
- **User Emotions**: Skepticism; cautious optimism as they evaluate potential solutions.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Offer comparison charts against competitors; provide detailed case studies demonstrating Clickforge's effectiveness.
- **Metric**: Number of demo requests and downloads of product documentation.

---

## Phase 3: Try
- **Trigger Event**: Users decide to test Clickforge after evaluating its features and benefits.
- **Friction Points**: Initial setup complexity; potential bugs or learning curve associated with new software.
- **User Emotions**: Excitement mixed with anxiety; hope that Clickforge will meet their needs.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide an intuitive onboarding process; offer live support during the trial period; create engaging tutorials and documentation.
- **Metric**: Trial conversion rate (percentage of users who move from trial to paid).

---

## Phase 4: Adopt
- **Trigger Event**: Users successfully complete the trial and decide to purchase Clickforge.
- **Friction Points**: Concerns about ongoing support and updates; integration with existing DevSecOps tools.
- **User Emotions**: Relief and satisfaction; confidence in their choice if the onboarding experience is smooth.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Ensure seamless integration with existing tools; provide personalized onboarding sessions and dedicated account managers.
- **Metric**: Customer retention rate and Net Promoter Score (NPS) post-adoption.

---

## Phase 5: Expand
- **Trigger Event**: Users recognize the value of Clickforge and seek to utilize it for more complex tasks or across teams.
- **Friction Points**: Limited knowledge of advanced features; potential resistance from team members to adopt new workflows.
- **User Emotions**: Enthusiasm for new capabilities; apprehension about change management within their teams.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Offer advanced training sessions and workshops; create a community forum for users to share best practices.
- **Metric**: Upsell rate (percentage of existing customers purchasing additional features or licenses).
```
